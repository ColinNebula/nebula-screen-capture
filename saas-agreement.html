4. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

4.1 Uptime Commitment

We commit to the following uptime targets:

  • Free Plan: 95% monthly uptime
  • Pro Plan: 99% monthly uptime
  • Premium Plan: 99.5% monthly uptime

4.2 Scheduled Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance windows are excluded from uptime calculations. We will provide at least 48 hours' notice for planned maintenance.

4.3 Performance Standards

  • Recording start latency: < 2 seconds
  • Video processing time: < 5 minutes for 1-hour recordings
  • Cloud upload speed: Dependent on your internet connection
  • API response time: < 500ms (95th percentile)

4.4 Service Credits

If we fail to meet uptime commitments, Pro and Premium users may request service credits:

Service credits must be requested within 30 days of the incident.
